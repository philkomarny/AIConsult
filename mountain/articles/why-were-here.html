Why We're Here

The mission behind the mountain

AI isn't coming. It's here. And most people are still waiting for permission to use it.

I've spent 30 years watching technology transform industries — sometimes gracefully, often chaotically. The pattern is always the same: the tools arrive before the playbooks. The people who figure it out first don't wait for instructions. They experiment. They share. They pull others up the mountain with them.

That's why this exists.

What We Actually Do

We solve problems. Real ones. The automation that saves your team 10 hours a week. The workflow that turns chaos into clarity. The AI integration that makes your business feel like it hired a dozen specialists overnight. Whether you're a solo founder or running a department of 500, the problems are surprisingly similar — and so are the solutions.

We build community. Our Discord isn't a support forum. It's a working group. People share what's working, what's broken, and what they're trying next. No gatekeeping. No paywalls on the good stuff. Just practitioners helping practitioners.

We go vertical. Generic AI advice is everywhere. We go deep on specific industries — healthcare, education, finance, creative — because the difference between "AI can help with that" and "here's exactly how to implement it in your context" is everything.

The Philosophy

I live at 9,000 feet for a reason. Up here, the air is thinner, the distractions are fewer, and the signal cuts through the noise. That's the approach: less hype, more utility. Less theory, more implementation.

The best time to start using AI was two years ago. The second best time is now. And you don't have to figure it out alone.
